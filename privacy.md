# RYNPS Bot Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 23 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the RYNPS Discord bot ("the Bot"), operated by ItsRynno ("the Owner"), handles information.

By using the Bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information the Bot May Access

Depending on the Bot's features and Discord permissions, the Bot may access or process:

* Discord user IDs
* Discord server IDs
* Discord channel IDs
* Discord role IDs
* Discord usernames and display names
* Messages or command content sent directly to the Bot
* Basic moderation, configuration, or game-related data needed for Bot features

The Bot does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment information, home addresses, or private messages outside of interactions directly involving the Bot.

## 2. How Information Is Used

Information is only used to provide and operate Bot features, including:

* Responding to commands
* Moderation and server management
* Saving server configuration settings
* Providing RYNPS community or Growtopia private server features
* Preventing abuse, exploits, spam, or misuse of the Bot

## 3. Data Storage

Some information may be stored where necessary for the Bot to function, such as server settings, moderation records, user IDs, or game-related account data.

Data is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Bot and its features.

## 4. Data Sharing

The Owner does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Information may only be shared where required by law, necessary to protect the Bot or its users, or required to comply with Discord's policies.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Bot operates through Discord and may use services required to host, operate, or improve Bot functionality.

Discord's own privacy practices are governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.

## 6. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of data associated with their Discord account by contacting the Owner through the RYNPS Discord server or by contacting ItsRynno on Discord.

Some data may need to be retained temporarily where required for moderation, security, fraud prevention, or legal reasons.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.
